Welcome to Make Every Child Happy

We are a youth-led non-profit company registered in South Africa aiming to support school learners with essentials such as sanitary pads, stationery, food parcels, and guidance through our programs.

About Us

Founded in 2024 by passionate TVET college students, Make Every Child Happy (NPC) was born out of a desire to bring dignity and opportunity to disadvantaged learners across KwaZulu-Natal. We operate purely on donations and volunteer efforts.
