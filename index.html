Welcome

We are a non-profit committed to supporting learners from underprivileged communities with basic necessities, educational guidance, and community support. Join us in building a brighter future for every child.

About Us

Make Every Child Happy NPC is founded by passionate students and community leaders committed to social impact and educational development. We operate in KwaZulu-Natal and serve schools across multiple townships.
